About us

Mush's Veterinary Clinic is a local clinic located at the heart of the capital city. We pride ourselves in our top quality service, ensuring that your beloved pets recieve the best care possible. Our staff consists of certified professionals with over 20 years of great service and positive reviews!

Meet our Team!


Stacey

Stacey has been with us for 6 years now. She graduated at Ontario's Vet College with honours and has been awarded with the Ottawa's Vet of the Year award in 2019. Stacey specializes in pet grooming and will always leave your pet looking fresh and clean.

Deborah

Deborah is our longest tenured vet, with 25 years of experience. She prides herself on her top quality customer service and always leaves both pets and their owners with a smile. Deborah is highly esteemed in this field and is on the board for theCMVA (Canadian Medical Veterinary Association).

Angela

Angela has been with us for 10 years now. She's done volunteer vet work in 6 different countries. Angela specializes in wound treatment. She always treats your pets with proper care, with minimal to no pain on the wound treatments, guaranteed.
